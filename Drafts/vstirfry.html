Vegan Stir Fry

Ingredients:

For Base

  • Cups Cooked rice
  • Onion, Chopped
  • Bell Peper, Sliced Thinly
  • Mushrooms
  • Cauliflower
  • Peas
  • Carrot, Grated

For Sauce

  • 3 parts Soy Sauce
  • 1 part Peanut Butter
  • 1 part Sesame Oil
  • Minced Garlic Clove to taste

Instructions:

  1. Begin by Chopping your Veggies- The amounts depend on how much rice youre making, and also personal preference. Get all your ingedients chopped and ready to go
  2. In a large frying pan, begin to sautee the vegatables starting with the onlion. Use water to sautee as we will be adding oil later.
  3. Add the Peppers next, then the rest of the Vegatbles. Let them simmer in the pan to cook and soften
  4. To make the sauce, simply whisk all the ngredients together in a bowl. Again, measurments are on how much rice you're making.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the frying pan and combine well with the vegatbes. Pour the sauce over the rice and continue to mix until all is well combined
  6. Let the rice simmer to let the sauce and flavors sink in. Serve with a sliced Avacado or some vegan scallion pancakes and enjoy!
